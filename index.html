Japanese Cuisine is the best!
One of the most fascinating aspects of Japanese cuisine is its deep connection to seasonality (shun, 旬) and the concept of "ichi-go ichi-e" (一期一会)—a philosophy that emphasizes cherishing each unique moment. These principles manifest in the way Japanese food is prepared, presented, and enjoyed.
Some Japanese food we all love
Onigiris, traditional Japanese rice balls or rice rolls
Nigiri, hand-pressed rice topped with slices of raw fish, seafood, or other ingredients
Maki, rolled sushi where rice and fillings are wrapped in a sheet of nori
Temaki, hand-rolled sushi in the shape of a cone, wrapped in nori and filled with rice and other ingredients
